About Us

COMFY SANCTUARY was founded by Charles Malinao in 1995 as a family business in downtown Vancouver in the western part of Canada. Its first store featured low-priced lookalike products of popular, higher-end clothing and fashion. Charles Malinao named COMFY SANCTUARY was inspired by from his good friend. In the next 8 years, Comfy Sanctuary approach towards fashion and its business model gradually generated traction with the Canadians consumer. This led to the opening of 9 new stores in the biggest cities of Canada.
